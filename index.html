What we do and what we build.

Landscape Design

We create professional custom landscape plans tailored to your property's unique character and your lifestyle needs. From plant selection and placement to hardscape integration, we design outdoor spaces that are both beautiful and functional. Our designs balance aesthetics with practical considerations like drainage, maintenance, and seasonal interest. Our plans are produced with CAD and ready to be submitted for permitting or HOA approval.

Grading

Grading is the foundation of everything that follows. We sculpt terrain to guide water naturally, create comfortable gathering areas, and reveal the best qualities of your property. Every slope, swale, and contour is intentionally engineered for function and formed for beauty. It's the invisible artistry that makes water disappear, lawns thrive, and outdoor spaces feel perfectly proportioned. The result is terrain that feels natural, functions flawlessly, and sets the stage for everything we build.

Drainage

In mountain terrain, water is powerful and persistent. We engineer drainage systems for steep terrain, variable soil, and heavy rainfall. French drains, graded swales, and Drain Gardens designed to manage the entire watershed of your property. Our experience in reading slope, calculating capacity, and building solutions protects structures and prevents erosion while turning drainage challenges into functional and attractive landscape features.

Plantings

From 4-inch pots to trees over 20 feet tall, we have the knowledge and equipment to put the right plant in the right place. We consider sun, soil, drainage and function for every planting. Privacy screens, layered borders, and foundation plantings are composed with care and attention to detail. With over 18 years of experience, we source quality plants at competitive rates and we install them right. The result is a landscape that serves your needs and looks good from installation to maturity.

Boulders

In mountain landscapes, stone tells a story. It carries weight and history, becoming part of the architecture of the land itself. We use boulders to shape space, manage grade transitions, and define natural edges within the landscape. Each boulder is carefully set for stability and proportion ‚Äî partially embedded and oriented to feel settled over time. The goal is not decoration, but harmony: stone that enhances the natural beauty of the property while adding quiet strength, depth, and balance.

Retaining Walls

Our retaining walls are both structural and sculptural. Built for the terrain and style of the mountains and designed to transform slopes into striking landscape features. We solve grade challenges while creating outdoor spaces that elevate your property.

Patios

A patio should feel like a natural extension of your home. We design and build custom patios that create comfortable, grounded spaces for gathering, dining, and quiet evenings outdoors. Using natural stone or concrete pavers, integrated seating walls, and thoughtfully placed boulders, each layout is shaped with attention to flow, proportion, and connection to the land. The result is an outdoor room that feels welcoming, balanced, and truly meant to be lived in.

Fire

Fire features define outdoor rooms. We craft custom fireplaces and fire pits that create natural gathering places. Whether it's a towering stone chimney or an intimate circle nestled in a patio. Stone hearths, built-in seating, and thoughtful placement turn your space into a destination with purpose and atmosphere. The result is an outdoor room with a view to the sky. A place where memories are forged.

Landscape Lighting

Landscape lighting adds a quiet layer of magic after sunset. We design and install low-voltage lighting systems that highlight architecture, illuminate pathways, and create depth in plantings. Strategic placement transforms how your property feels after dark‚Äîsafe, usable, and layered with ambiance.

Steps and Pathways

Elevation changes are part of mountain living. We build steps and pathways that flow with the terrain, creating smooth transitions across slopes and between outdoor rooms. Every line is shaped with care for movement, safety, and proportion. The result is structure without rigidity and defined routes that feel natural, balanced, and fully integrated into the land.

About

Elemental Landscapes is a fully licensed and insured landscape contractor serving Western North Carolina.

We specialize in landscape design and construction, offering services such as retaining walls, grading, mulching, landscape lighting, paving, water features, planting, sod installation, outdoor kitchens, artificial turf, and patios.

We are a Veteran-owned business with over 12 years of experience in landscape construction and design.
